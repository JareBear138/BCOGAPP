Prayer Request Privacy Info:


All Prayer requests are kept private by default. This means that your request will not be published in the bulletin or sent through the prayer chain. You can allow your request to be published by checking "Publish my request" when submitting your request through the app. If you check this option, another option for publishing the request on the app will be displayed. Please also check this option if you wish for your request to be published on the app.


Remember that the app is available to the public. Even though the request is public, we still encourage you to share it on the app if you can. Publishing your request on the app makes it easy for anyone to pray for your request at anytime from anywhere in the world with an Internet connection. If you do decide to publish your request on the app, you can protect your privacy by limiting personal information, instead opting to replace names with titles or how you are related to the subject. Ex: My Father's Health or My Friend is Moving. Generally, we will publish your name, so continuing with the above examples we will publish something like: Robert Smith's Father's Health, or Christopher Rice's friend is moving.


It is important to note that your requests are never automatically published, but are viewed by the Church Office and Pastoral Staff first. If we think that you have made a mistake, or if we believe that you accidentally chose to publish a very personal request, we will reach out to you. So make sure that your name and email are correct so we can contact you. If you wish to take down, change, or stop your request from being published, contact us at the Church office: (Phone: (716)-487-1636) (Email: office@busticog.org). You can include any stipulations regarding the privacy of your request within your prayer request. We look forward to hearing from you and praying for your needs.

Sunday Services:


8:00 am Traditional Service: Open to people of all ages. This service offers a more intimate worship experience led by a worship team of singers and instrumentalists with a more traditional feel. We sing mostly hymns. Pastor Roy Ferguson leads the teaching time.


10:45 am Contemporary Service: Open to people of all ages. This service has a more contemporary edge to it with a worship band and vocalists. Pastor Roy leads the teaching time. Children's programs during this service - KidZone: creates a safe, yet high-energy, interactive environment for kids to learn about God.


Rejoicing Spirits: On the first Sunday of every month, at 4:00 pm, we have a worship service designed to engage individuals with developmental disabilities. The goal is to reach out and encourage people with developmental disabilities to become part of a faith community. The service is adapted to engage the attention and interest of individuals with special needs along with their caregivers, families, and other supportive members in the community. The environment is accepting and welcoming to all children of God.
